Loopin' Space


Controls

Arrow keys / WASD : Move ship

Left click : Hold to charge, release to launch

F11 : Full Screen Mode



Pro Tips

You can't move while your engine is launched, so be careful with your positioning !

The more you hold the fire button, the fastest the engine will travel.

While yellow ennemy move in a straight line, the purple one will chase you.

Try to (unlock condition) to fight the boss !


Press fire to start the game
